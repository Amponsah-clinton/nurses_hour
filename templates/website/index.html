Study tips & updates

Ways to study smarter

Tip

Time-block your prep

Schedule fixed study slots and stick to them. Short, regular sessions beat last-minute cramming and help you retain more for exam day.

Tip

Review every explanation

After each practice question, read the full explanation—right or wrong. Understanding the “why” builds the judgment you need on the exam.

Tip

Simulate exam conditions

Do timed blocks in a quiet space, no notes. Getting used to pressure and pace now will help you stay calm when it counts.
